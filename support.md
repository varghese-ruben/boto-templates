## Common questions

**The camera screen says "Camera access needed."**
Boto needs camera permission to capture photos. Open iOS Settings → Boto Camera → toggle Camera on, then re-open Boto.

**My photos aren't appearing in the iOS Photos app.**
Open iOS Settings → Boto Camera → Photos → choose either "Add Only" or "Full Access". Boto only needs Add Only. Photos you've already taken before granting access stay in the app's Gallery — only new captures get added to Photos.

**The Templates tab shows "Catalog unavailable" or "You're offline."**
Boto fetches its newest templates from a public CDN. If you're offline (or the CDN is briefly unreachable), the app uses the built-in template set that ships with the app. Everything still works — you just don't see the very latest additions. Reopen the app once you're back online and the catalog refreshes automatically.

**How do I delete a template I created?**
Templates tab → tap _My Templates_ → long-press a template → Delete Template. To wipe all of them at once: Settings → Templates → Reset user templates.

**How do I free up storage?**
Settings shows total photo storage used. To clear remote template image cache: Settings → Storage → Clear remote template cache. To delete individual photos: Gallery → tap a photo → Trash icon.

**Will my photos sync between devices?**
No. Boto stores photos on the device that captured them. If you saved them to your iOS Photos library (the default), those are synced by iCloud Photos like any other photo.
